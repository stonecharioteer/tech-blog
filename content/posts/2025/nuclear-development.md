## How I Use AI

I live in the terminal. I've used Linux for about 20 years now, and I moved from
Sublime Text to VS Code to Vim to Neovim. I'm happy here.

In May, I was working with a company where everyone asked me why I don't use
Cursor, or Windsurf. My short answer was "I hate VS Code". But the long answer
is that I love `tmux` and `neovim`, but mostly `tmux`. I've been using tmux for
about 6 years and I really love it. I've also been using a tiling window manager
on and off for 4 years, but that's another post one day.
